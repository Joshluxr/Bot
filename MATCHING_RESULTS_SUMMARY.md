## Analysis

### Why No Matches?

The lack of matches could be due to several factors:

1. **Search Space Size**
   - Bitcoin has 2^160 possible addresses (~1.46 × 10^48)
   - Only 55.4M addresses have ever been funded (~0.000000000000000000000000000000000000000004% of total)
   - Even with 262K candidates, probability of collision is extremely low

2. **Search Strategy**
   - The GPU servers may be searching a specific range or pattern
   - The search might not be targeting known funded addresses
   - Random search in Bitcoin address space is not effective without targeting

3. **Address Format**
   - Mismatch between candidate format and database format (P2PKH vs P2SH vs Bech32)
   - Candidates might be public keys rather than addresses

### Recommendations

1. **Verify Candidate Format**
   - Check if candidates are in correct Bitcoin address format
   - Ensure format matches database (check first few lines of each file)

2. **Review Search Strategy**
   - Current brute force approach is statistically unlikely to find matches
   - Consider targeted search if looking for specific addresses
   - Review the GPU search parameters and range

3. **Expected Success Rate**
   - With random search: effectively 0% chance of finding funded addresses
   - Even with optimized search: extremely low probability without specific targets
